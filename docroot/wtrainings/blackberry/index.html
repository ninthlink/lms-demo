What is Snapdragon™ Processor?

Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ all-in-one processors give you technologies that work faster, smoother, and more efficiently. That’s because everything is designed to work together, delivering the ultimate mobile experience.

What Makes Snapdragon Special?

The components on Snapdragon processors — CPU, GPU, modem, GPS, multimedia and other technologies — are built together from the ground up, meaning that Qualcomm designs, integrates and delivers a complete system-on-a-chip.

The entire system is purpose-built and integrated to work together. It delivers a great mobile experience with extended battery life on BlackBerry® 10 devices - no matter what you’re doing.

Blackberry® Hub & BBM Video with Screen Share

The Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ processor inside BlackBerry 10 devices includes Qualcomm's own custom-designed GPU and CPU, providing the processing power to deliver dazzling, complex, graphics and rich UIs that respond immediately to user interaction while maintaining power efficiency.

Advanced compositing and fast graphics rendering ensure smooth, flicker-free animation. And the entire SoC works seamlessly to deliver 1080p HD 30FPS video capture and playback, taking the sharing experience to another level and helping you to get a closer to real-life conversation with BBM video calls.
