Snapdragon 800 Processor

Snapdragon 800 is the rock star in the Snapdragon line-up. It's designed for high-end smartphones, tablets, Smart TVs and digital media players. It’s ideal for the “cutting edge” customer. Talk about:

Features

4K Ultra HD video playback and 7.1 surround sound

Powerful graphic performance for responsive gaming

Powerful connectivity with 4G LTE, Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth 4.0

View Product Brief

Snapdragon 800 Customer: Lives on the Edge

  • Loves gaming, entertainment and HD video chat
  • Into high-end devices and know a lot about tech
  • Use device for 6+ hours a day

Selling Tip: This customer has high expectations. Be ready to dive in deep on features and technologies.

Snapdragon 600 Processor

Snapdragon 600 is designed for customers who use their devices for a lot of entertainment like movies and gaming. With this customer you might talk about:

Features

Responsive web browsing, scrolling and zooming

Smooth video capture, playback and upload

Reliable connections with 4G LTE, Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth 4.0

View Product Brief

Snapdragon 600 Customer: Appreciates All Around Performance

  • Heavy web browsing, social networking
  • Camera, video and connectivity for fast uploads are important
  • Use device for 4+ hours a day

Selling Tip: This customer needs all-around performance, but might be interested in a Snapdragon 800 device if they are a movie enthusiast.

Snapdragon 400 Processor

Snapdragon 400 is designed for people who are constantly connected through social media and online communication. With this customer you talk about:

Features

Responsive web browsing, scrolling and zooming

Reliable connections with 4G LTE, Wi-Fi 802.11ac, Bluetooth 4.0

Great voice and data connectivity

View Product Brief

Snapdragon 400 Customer: In the Mainstream

  • Light web browsing, email and social networking
  • May listen to music or watch online video
  • Use device for 4+ hours a day

Selling Tip: This is the mainstream customer. Show them social media, email and entertainment capabilities.

Snapdragon 200 Processor

Snapdragon 200 is designed for people that want a quality smartphone at a great price. With this customer talk about:

Features

Auto Volume Mode makes voice calling easier

Apps for music playback and GPS are usually pre-installed

Great graphics performance for web browsing and social media

View Product Brief

Snapdragon 200 Customer: Just the Basics

  • Uses the basics like voice and texting
  • Uses basic apps like music, maps and email
  • Use device for about 1 hour a day

Selling Tip: This customer wants a quality smartphone at a value price. Don’t get technical. Show them the basics and emphasize simplicity of use.
