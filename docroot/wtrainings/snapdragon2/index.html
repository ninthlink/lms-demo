Qualcomm® Snapdragon Processors

Qualcomm Snapdragon processors give your mobile devices breathtaking speed, jaw-dropping graphics and longer battery life so you can do more of the things you love.

"If you've got any smartphone chances are pretty good that it's powered by one of Qualcomm's Snapdragon chipsets." — TechCrunch

There are over 1000 Snapdragon devices announced/commercially available including more than 40 tablets. In addition to enabling a large number of Android-powered smartphones, Qualcomm powers all Windows Phones available today. Cumulatively, over 11 billion chips have been shipped to date.

Qualcomm is the world leader in mobile technologies.

Qualcomm innovates and manufactures breakthrough mobile chipsets enabling partners around the world to create next-gen mobile devices, services, and experiences. Qualcomm is making wireless more personal, affordable, and accessible to people everywhere.

"Qualcomm's reach is global and unsurpassed. They are designed into everywhere that matters." — Businessweek

The part to look for is the part you can't see.

Roll over each icon to learn more

Multimedia: Whether you want to capture high-quality photos and videos or watch movies in HD, Qualcomm's audio and video technology lets you do more.

Power Management: Snapdragon has best-in-class technologies designed and built to work together so that your devices work more efficiently. Now you can do more, and recharge less.

Radio Frequency (RF): Support for a wide frequency spectrum to ensure that your device is connected no matter where you are.

Location: Snapdragon processors are first to deliver GPS plus GLONASS for more accurate and reliable positioning.

Modem: Snapdragon processors offer a comprehensive cellular multimode modem solution with integrated support for 4G LTE and 3G to provide a consistent, seamless user experience.

Connectivity: Ubiquitous, real-time connectivity including WWAN, Wi-Fi®, and Bluetooth® technology.

Memory: Memory is essential for all computing needs, from multi-tab web browsing to 3D gaming.

Digital Signal Processor (DSP): Dedicated Sub-processors that help take the load off of the CPU and GPU, freeing up valuable resources and helping preserve battery life.

Central Processing Unit (CPU): The 'brain' of the Snapdragon processor is the Scorpion CPU, which Qualcomm custom designs for mobile. Snapdragon CPU's are performance optimized, allowing fantastic user experiences without draining your battery. Qualcomm's S1 included the first mobile CPU to reach 1GHz speed and the latest S4 leads the pack with the first mobile processor to hit 1.5GHz.

Graphics Processing Unit (GPU): The Snapdragon chipset and its integrated Adreno GPU together enable power-efficient, high-end 3D graphics and console quality gaming across a broad range of mobile devices and on even the largest, highest resolution displays.

Operating System (OS): Our processors support operating systems that run on the majority of mobile devices, including Android and Windows Phone.
