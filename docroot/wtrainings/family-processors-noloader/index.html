Snapdragon 200 processors Overview

Designed to make exceptional performance and connectivity more accessible than ever.

  • Strong connections with 3G, Wi-Fi 802.11n, Bluetooth 4.0, USB 2.0
  • Crystal Clear Voice – Experience voice clarity even in the noisiest environments. Extend talk time with conversations that are pleasant and free of ambient noise
  • Battery life – Like every other processor in our Snapdragon family, these are designed to deliver excellent battery efficiency
