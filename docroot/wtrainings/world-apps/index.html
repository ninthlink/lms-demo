The World of Apps

Introduction

For your customer, the question they have about a smartphone isn't, "How does it work?" it's "What can it do for me?

Learn how apps can turn "a smartphone" into "my smartphone."

What are Apps?

An app (short for application) is software that performs a specific task - like interacting with Facebook, checking the weather, playing music or editing and sharing photos. Apps can turn a standard device into a tool that uniquely your own.

Where Do Apps Come From?

There are four major platforms and each has their own marketplace where users can buy and download apps specifically for their device.

Why Do Apps Matter?

Apps personalize a smartphone, so while customers might own identical devices, they will use them in different ways.

  • Business Travelers might be interested in weather, budgeting and finance, airline and maps apps.
  • Sports Fans might want apps for league news, scores, and game schedules.
  • An Aspiring Chef might have apps to store recipes, photograph dishes, read or create reviews of local restaurants.

Let's look at some popular categories.

Entertainment

With Entertainment apps, you can enjoy content - or create your own. Music. Video. Books. Whether you're looking to be entertained or do the entertaining, there's an app to help make it happen.

(Google Play Store top free entertainment apps in the U.S. on 9/25/13)

(Google Play Store top games apps in the U.S. on 10/2/13)

Games

Gaming apps include casual time-passers and immersive console-quality gaming experiences. Playing games on a smartphone can happen anywhere, anytime – no sofa required. Whether you want to just play a word game with friends for a few minutes or you're a hard-core gamer up for an all-nighter, there's something for everybody.

Productivity

Productivity apps help you get stuff done – like making lists, tracking time, writing notes or creating a budget. Many store data in the cloud, or communicate directly with your computer so your information is always available.

(Windows Phone store top paid tools + productivity apps in the U.S. on 10/2/13)

Hobbies & Lifestyle

Apps keep things you care about closeby. Journal thoughts, capture inspirational snapshots, shop, and tune in to content and information. Or, customize your device with backgrounds or icons that suit your passion.

(Google Play Store top lifestyle apps in the U.S. on 9/25/13)

(BlackBerry World top social apps in the U.S. on 9/25/13)

Social Networking

One of the most popular uses for a smartphone is staying in touch - and this is an area your customer will probably be very interested in. Social networking apps let you update your status, check-in at favorite spots and broadcast spontaneous photos or videos.

(Google Play store top health & fitness apps in the U.S. on 10/2/13)

Health & Fitness

Apps can help track what you're eating, motivate with a workout and encouragement, track your mileage, pace, and then guide you to a good night's sleep. Whether you're looking to shed a few pounds or train for a marathon, apps can help get you on the road to a healthy life.

Education

A smartphone can a constant educational companion - giving you access to so many learning tools. Learn a new skill or language. Explore the human body, a far-off country, or virtually dissect a frog. Apps can take you from "curious" to "expert".

(Windows Phone store top paid education apps in the U.S. on 9/25/13)

Kids

From the amusing to the educational, kids apps deliver a whole new kind of interactive experience. They can see their favorite character come to life on the smartphone. From learning their ABC's to solving puzzles, kids apps turn your smartphone into a virtual playground.

(Windows Phone store top paid kids + family apps in the U.S. on 10/2/13)

Unique… just like you.

Apps are what make your smartphone, well, yours. And with so many different kinds of apps available you can make your standard device into something that’s truly personal.
